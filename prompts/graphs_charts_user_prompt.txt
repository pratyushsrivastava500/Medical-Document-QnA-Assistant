**Format for each chart:**
```
Chart [Number]: [Chart Type] - [Title]

[ASCII representation or data table]

Explanation:
[2-3 sentences explaining what this shows and its significance]
```

**Example formats you can use:**

For trends over time:
```
Chart 1: Line Chart - Blood Glucose Levels Over 6 Months

Jan  Feb  Mar  Apr  May  Jun
150  145  140  135  130  125 mg/dL
 |    ↘    ↘    ↘    ↘    ↘

Explanation: Blood glucose levels showed consistent improvement from 150 mg/dL in January to 125 mg/dL in June, indicating effective diabetes management.
```

For comparisons:
```
Chart 2: Bar Chart - Treatment Outcomes

Group A (Treatment): ████████████ 85% success
Group B (Control):   ██████ 45% success

Explanation: Patients receiving the treatment showed significantly higher success rates (85%) compared to the control group (45%).
```

```

| Measurement    | Value     | Range      | Status  |
```
